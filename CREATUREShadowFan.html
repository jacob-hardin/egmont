Shadow Fans

Dreamlands Creature Profile

Description: A whispering, faceless throng of dream-people who obsessively adore the dreamer—until they smother, drain, or absorb them. The more attention they're given, the more real they become.

Special Abilities

  • Suffocating Attention: Failing a CON × 5 roll after contact begins results in suffocation (1d6 damage/round after 1d4 rounds).
  • Steal the Self: POW contest after 3 rounds of contact. On failure: lose SAN and stats. Complete loss = transformation into Shadow Fan.
  • Divide and Echo: Each time the target speaks their name, signs something, or engages with the Fans, they duplicate (1d4 HP restored, +5% to attacks).
  • Dreamform: Immune to normal weapons unless enchanted, dream-based, or fire. Physical damage is halved.

Behavior & Tactics

  • Approaches quietly and surrounds targets. Avoids speed, favors omnipresence.
  • Appears in dreams, mirrors, or performance stages.
  • If ignored and resisted without speech, they may fade. If acknowledged, they become stronger.

Plot Hooks

  • A dreamer has gone missing, last seen performing in a dream-arena full of whispering fans.
  • Shadow Fans leak into the waking world through mirrors and old recordings.
  • A famous dreamer offers help, but they’ve been replaced by a whispering copy.

Note: The more the players engage with the Shadow Fans—emotionally, verbally, or physically—the stronger and more invasive the Fans become. Escaping them requires total disengagement or violent dreamlogic resistance.
