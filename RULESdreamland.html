Dreamlands GM Reference Sheet

Call of Cthulhu (7th Edition)

Success Levels

Success Type Roll Needed Description
Regular ≤ Skill Success under normal conditions.
Hard ≤ Half Skill Challenging or high-stress situations.
Extreme ≤ One-fifth Skill Nearly impossible tasks or Mythos interference.
Critical Success 01 Flawless success with narrative impact.
Fumble 96–100 (if skill < 50) Catastrophic failure.

Difficulty Guidelines (Dreamlands Context)

Situation Suggested Difficulty Example
Normal Task (well-lit dreamroom) Regular Reading an ancient map in Ulthar.
High Stress or Warped Logic Hard Fighting a Zoog in shifting fog.
Supernatural Impossibility Extreme Balancing on a spinning moonbeam or resisting a Gugs roar.

Bonus and Penalty Dice

Modifier When to Use Effect
Bonus Die Good tools, help, dreaming clarity Roll 2 tens dice, take the lower.
Penalty Die Distraction, injury, hostile terrain Roll 2 tens dice, take the higher.
Keeper Tip: Dreamlands difficulty should reflect not just physical challenge, but also surreal logic. Reward creative, symbolic, or emotional solutions as much as mechanical success.
